# Inventra — End-user help guide and FAQ

**Includes:** Pilot onboarding packet, feature walkthroughs, and frequently asked questions (FAQ)
**Version:** 2.0 · June 2026

## Part 1: Pilot onboarding packet

Welcome to the Inventra pilot programme. This packet gets you started in under ten minutes.

### What is Inventra?

Inventra is a business app that helps you:

- Know exactly what stock you have, from your phone
- Record sales quickly
- Manage your suppliers and purchase orders
- Check your real stock against your records with a stock count
- Get a warning before any item runs out
- See what each staff member has sold
- Generate a sales report at the end of the week or month

If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Inventra.

### What you need to get started

- A smartphone
- Your phone number
- Your business name

### Your quick start

1. Open Inventra and tap **Create new business account**.
2. Enter your phone number. We send you a one-time password (OTP); enter it to confirm.
3. Enter your business name.
4. Tap **Go to my dashboard**. You are in.

> **Tip:** For quick sign-in on a shared counter phone, set up a six-digit personal identification number (PIN) in Settings.

### Staff roles

| Role | Who it is for | What they can do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Admin (owner) | The business owner | Everything: all features, reports, users, and settings |
| Manager | A trusted supervisor | Record sales, manage stock and suppliers, run stock counts, view reports. Cannot manage users. |
| Attendant | Counter staff | Record sales, view their own sales, and enter counts during a stock count. Cannot see financial reports. |

## Part 2: Feature walkthroughs

### 1. Manage your products

**Add a product:** Tap **Inventory**, tap the **+** button, fill in the details (name, category, cost price, selling price, opening stock, reorder level), and tap **Save product**. Tap **More options** to add an expiry date.

**Record stock arrivals (stock-in):** Open the product, tap **Add stock**, enter the quantity received, optionally add a note, and tap **Confirm**.

**Remove stock manually (stock-out):** Open the product, tap **Adjust stock**, select **Decrease**, enter the quantity and a reason, and tap **Confirm**.

### 2. Manage suppliers and purchase orders

**Add a supplier:** Go to **Suppliers**, tap **Add supplier**, enter the name and contact details, and save.

**Create a purchase order:** Go to **Purchase orders**, tap **New order**, choose the supplier and items, and save. You can update the order's status as it moves toward delivery, and delete an order if needed.

### 3. Record sales

**Single-item sale:** From the dashboard, tap **Record sale**, search for the product, check the quantity, and tap **Confirm sale**.

**Multiple items:** Add the first item, tap **Add another item**, add more products, then tap **Confirm sale**.

**Void a sale (Manager or Admin only):** Go to **Sales**, open the sale, tap **Void sale**, enter a reason, and confirm. The original sale stays in the record marked as voided, and the stock is returned.

### 4. Alerts

Inventra fires a **low-stock alert** when stock reaches or falls below your reorder level, and an **expiry alert** before a product's expiry date. Tap the **Alerts** icon to review and act on each one.

### 5. Run a stock count (reconciliation)

A stock count checks whether what Inventra says you have matches what is actually on your shelf, and shows what any difference is worth.

1. Tap **Reconciliation** and tap **New Count**.
2. For each product, the screen shows the system count. Count the item and enter the physical count.
3. Inventra shows the difference and labels each item **Match**, **Loss**, or **Surplus**, and shows the value impact in Naira.
4. Tap an item to open the discrepancy details, review or add a reason, then choose **Approve Adjustment** (updates your stock, after a confirmation prompt) or **Request Recount**.

### 6. Reports

Tap **Reports**, select the report type and date range, tap **Preview**, then **Export** and choose a format. The file appears in your phone's share menu, so you can save it or send it by WhatsApp or email.

## Part 3: Frequently asked questions

**How long does onboarding take?** From sign-up to recording your first product takes under ten minutes.

**Can I use Inventra on more than one device?** Multiple-device support is part of the Premium plan.

**Can I add suppliers and create purchase orders?** Yes. Use the Suppliers and Purchase orders sections.

**Can my attendant see financial reports?** No. Attendants see only their own sales. Reports and revenue summaries are visible to Admin and Manager roles only.

**A staff member left. How do I remove their access?** Go to Settings, Manage staff, find the person, and tap **Deactivate**. Access is revoked immediately; their past sales stay in the records.

**Can I check my actual stock against what the app says?** Yes. Use Reconciliation to run a stock count.

**I forgot my password.** On the login screen, tap **Forgot password** and follow the steps, including the one-time password sent to your phone.
