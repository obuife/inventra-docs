# PART 1: PILOT USER ONBOARDING PACKET

Welcome to the StockSense pilot programme! You have been selected as one of our first business owners to use StockSense before it officially launches. Thank you for your time and trust.

This packet will get you started in under 10 minutes.

---

## What is StockSense?

StockSense is a business app that helps you:
- Know exactly what stock you have, at any time, from your phone
- Record sales quickly — even without internet
- Get a warning before any item runs out
- See what each staff member has sold
- Generate a sales report at the end of the week or month

If you can use WhatsApp, you can use StockSense.

---

## What You Need to Get Started

- An Android smartphone (any brand — Tecno, Samsung, Infinix, Itel, etc.)
- Android version 8.0 or higher
- Your Nigerian phone number
- Your business name

---

## Your 5-Step Quick Start

1. Download the StockSense app from the link in your welcome SMS, or open the web address in Chrome.
2. Tap **"Create new business account."**
3. Enter your phone number. You will receive an SMS with a 6-digit code. Enter it within 10 minutes.
4. Enter your business name and create a password (at least 8 characters with one number).
5. Tap **"Go to my Dashboard."** You're in!

> ✅ **Tip:** Your first task is to add your products. The app guides you with a checklist on your dashboard.

---

## Your Dashboard Explained

| Section | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Greeting** | Your business name and a count of items that need attention today |
| **Today's Sales** | Total money received today and number of transactions |

## Adding Your Staff

Go to **Settings > Manage Staff > Invite User**. Enter their phone number and select their role. They'll receive an SMS with a link to set up their account.

### Staff Roles

| Role | Who Is This For | What They Can Do |
|---|---|---|
| **Admin (Owner)** | You — the business owner | Everything: full access to all features, reports, users, and settings |
| **Manager** | A trusted supervisor | Record sales, manage stock and suppliers, view reports. Cannot manage users. |
| **Attendant** | Counter staff | Record sales and view their own sales only. Cannot see financial reports. |

## 1. Managing Your Products

### 1.1 Adding a New Product

1. From the bottom menu, tap **Inventory**.
2. Tap the green **+** button in the bottom-right corner.
3. Fill in the product details:
   - **Product Name** *(required)* — e.g., Indomie Chicken 70g
   - **Category** — choose from the list or create your own
   - **Cost Price** — what you paid the supplier
   - **Selling Price** — what you charge the customer
   - **Opening Stock** — how many you have right now
   - **Reorder Level** — the quantity at which you want to be alerted (e.g., 5)
4. Tap **Save Product**.

> ✅ Tap **More Options** to add an expiry date (required for pharmacies) or link the product to a supplier.

> ⚠️ If you set the Reorder Level to 0, you will **not** receive low-stock alerts for that product.

### 1.2 Recording Stock Arrivals (Stock In)

When a supplier delivers goods, record the new stock immediately.

1. Go to **Inventory** and find the product.
2. Tap the product to open its detail page.
3. Tap **Add Stock**.
4. Enter the quantity received.
5. Optionally add a note (e.g., supplier invoice number).
6. Tap **Confirm**.

> ✅ This works offline. The stock count updates on your phone immediately and syncs to the cloud when your internet returns.

### 1.3 Manually Removing Stock (Stock Out)

For stock removed for reasons other than a customer sale (damaged goods, expiry removal, personal use):

1. Go to **Inventory** and find the product.
2. Tap the product, then tap **Adjust Stock**.
3. Select **Decrease** and enter the quantity removed.
4. Select a reason from the list.
5. Tap **Confirm**.

> ⚠️ Stock Out records are permanent and logged with your name and the time.

## 2. Recording Sales

### 2.1 Recording a Single-Item Sale (3 taps)

1. From the Dashboard, tap **Record Sale**.
2. Type the product name in the search bar. Tap the product when it appears.
3. Check the quantity (defaults to 1). Change if needed.
4. Tap **Confirm Sale**.

> ✅ A green tick with *"Sale Recorded"* will appear. The screen clears immediately for the next sale.

### 2.2 Recording Multiple Items in One Sale

1. Add the first item (steps 1–3 above).
2. Instead of confirming, tap **Add Another Item**.
3. Search and add more products to the cart.
4. When all items are added, tap **Confirm Sale**.

### 2.3 Voiding an Incorrect Sale

*(Manager or Admin only)*

1. Go to **Sales** in the bottom menu.
2. Find the incorrect sale and tap it.
3. Tap **Void Sale**.
4. Enter a reason *(required)*.
5. Tap **Confirm Void**.

> ⚠️ Void is not delete. The original sale stays in the record marked as voided, and the stock is returned.

---

## 3. Alerts & Reordering

### 3.1 Types of Alerts

- **Low-Stock Alert:** fires when stock reaches or falls below your reorder level
- **Expiry Alert:** fires at 30, 14, and 7 days before a product's expiry date

### 3.2 Acting on a Low-Stock Alert

1. Tap the **Alerts** icon in the bottom menu.
2. Find the low-stock alert and tap it.
3. Tap **Create Purchase Order** — it pre-fills with product and supplier details.

> ✅ Tap **Dismiss** if you've already reordered elsewhere. The alert won't reappear until stock changes again.

### 3.3 Setting or Changing a Reorder Level

1. Go to **Inventory** and find the product.
2. Tap the product, then tap **Edit Product**.
3. Change the **Reorder Level** field.
4. Tap **Save**.

---

## 4. Supplier Management

### 4.1 Adding a Supplier

1. From the bottom menu, tap **More > Suppliers**.
2. Tap **Add Supplier**.
3. Enter the supplier name and phone number *(required)*.
4. Tap **Save**.

### 4.2 Creating a Purchase Order

1. Go to **More > Suppliers** and select the supplier.
2. Tap **New Purchase Order**.
3. Add the products and quantities you want to order.
4. Tap **Create Order** (status: Draft).
5. When ready, tap **Send Order**.

### 4.3 Confirming a Delivery

1. Go to **More > Suppliers > Order History**.
2. Find the relevant purchase order.
3. Tap **Confirm Delivery**.
4. Enter the actual quantity received.
5. Tap **Confirm**. Stock updates automatically.

---

## 5. Stock Reconciliation

Reconciliation lets you compare what StockSense says you have against what is physically on your shelf.

### Running a Stock Count

1. Go to **More > Reconciliation**.
2. Tap **Start New Count**.
3. Walk your shop and enter the actual quantity for each product.
4. StockSense shows the variance and its value in Naira as you go.
5. Tap **Finalize Count**.
6. For each variance, choose **Accept** (updates system stock) or **Flag for Investigation**.

> ✅ Works fully offline.

> ⚠️ Negative variance means stock is missing. A Naira value is shown to help you understand the financial impact.

---

## 6. Reports

### Viewing and Exporting a Report

1. Tap **Reports** in the bottom menu.
2. Select the report type: Sales, Inventory, Attendant Performance, or Audit Log.
3. Choose a date range (default: current week).
4. Tap **Preview** to see the report.
5. Tap **Export** → choose **PDF** or **CSV**.
6. The file appears in your phone's share menu — save it, or send via WhatsApp or email.

> ✅ PDF is great for accountants. CSV is useful for Excel.

---

## 7. Using StockSense Offline

| Action | What Happens Offline |
|---|---|
| Record a sale | Saved on your phone immediately. Badge shows pending sync. |
| Add stock | Updates locally. Syncs when internet returns. |
| Browse products | Full product list available from local storage. |
| View alerts | Alerts from your last sync are visible. |
| Generate reports | Uses data from last sync. Recent pending data shown after sync. |

When your internet returns, StockSense automatically syncs all pending actions in the background.

# PART 3: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

---

## Getting Started

**Q: I don't have a data connection. Can I still use StockSense?**
A: Yes. StockSense is built to work without internet. You can record sales, add stock, and browse your products offline. Everything syncs when your internet comes back.

**Q: How long does onboarding take?**
A: From download to recording your first product should take under 10 minutes. Recording your first sale after that takes under 30 seconds.

**Q: Can I use StockSense on a tablet or computer?**
A: Yes. Open the StockSense web address in Chrome on any device. On Android smartphones, you can also install it as an app.

---

## Products & Inventory

**Q: I have hundreds of products. Do I have to add them one by one?**
A: In the current version, yes. Bulk import via Excel/CSV is planned for Phase 2. Start with your highest-turnover products first.

**Q: What is the Reorder Level?**
A: The quantity at which StockSense alerts you to reorder. For example, if set to 5, you get an alert when only 5 units remain. Set it based on your supplier's delivery lead time.

**Q: Can I delete a product?**
A: Yes, but it's archived — not permanently removed. All sales and stock history are preserved.

**Q: My stock count went to zero but I can see goods on the shelf. What happened?**
A: A sale or adjustment was likely recorded incorrectly, or there is a pending offline sync. Go to **Inventory > [Product] > Stock History** to review all movements, then run a Reconciliation to correct the count.

---

## Sales

**Q: My attendant recorded the wrong quantity. What do I do?**
A: A Manager or Admin can void the incorrect sale and re-record it correctly. Go to **Sales**, find the sale, tap it, and select **Void Sale**.

**Q: Can my attendant see financial reports?**
A: No. Attendants only see their own sales. Reports and revenue summaries are only visible to Admin and Manager roles.

**Q: I sold something offline. Has the sale been recorded?**
A: Yes. StockSense saves every sale to your phone immediately. The sync badge disappears when it's uploaded to the cloud.

---

## Staff & Permissions

**Q: I want staff to record sales but not see my profits. Is that possible?**
A: Yes. Assign them the **Attendant** role.

**Q: A staff member left. How do I remove their access?**
A: Go to **Settings > Manage Staff**, find the staff member, and tap **Deactivate**. Access is revoked immediately.

---

## Alerts

**Q: I dismissed a low-stock alert but the item is still low. Will I be alerted again?**
A: Not until the stock level changes again. To see all low-stock items anytime, go to **Inventory** and filter by **Low Stock**.

**Q: My reorder level was 0 and I'm not getting alerts. Why?**
A: Setting the Reorder Level to 0 disables alerts for that product. Go to the product, tap **Edit**, and set a level above 0.

---

## Reports

**Q: Can I share a report with my accountant?**
A: Yes. Generate the report, tap **Export → PDF**, then use the share button to send it via WhatsApp or email.

---

## Technical & App Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**. Enter your phone number and you'll receive a 6-digit OTP via SMS.

**Q: My data is not syncing. What should I check?**
A: Check that you have an active data or WiFi connection, the connectivity bar shows **Online**, and there are no **Sync Failed** items. If syncing still fails, tap the sync badge to manually retry.
