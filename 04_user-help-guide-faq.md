# Part 1: Pilot user onboarding packet

Welcome to the Inventra pilot programme. You're one of our first business owners to use Inventra before it launches publicly. Thank you for your time and trust.

This packet gets you started in under 10 minutes.

---

## What is Inventra?

Inventra is a business app that helps you:

- Know exactly what stock you have, at any time, from your phone
- Record sales quickly
- Check your real stock against your records with a stock count
- Get a warning before any item runs out
- See what each staff member has sold
- Generate a sales report at the end of the week or month

If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Inventra.

---

## What you need to get started

- An Android smartphone (any brand — Tecno, Samsung, Infinix, Itel, and so on)
- Android version 8.0 or higher
- Your email address
- Your business name

---

## Your 5-step quick start

1. Download the Inventra app from the link in your welcome message, or open the web address in Chrome.
2. Tap **Create new business account**.
3. Enter your email address and create a password (at least 8 characters with one number).
4. Enter your business name.
5. Tap **Go to my dashboard**. You're in.

> **Tip:** Your first task is to add your products. The app guides you with a checklist on your dashboard.

> **Coming soon:** Registration and password reset by SMS one-time code are on the way. For now, use your email and password.

## Your dashboard explained

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Greeting | Your business name and a count of items that need attention today |
| Today's sales | Total money received today and the number of transactions |
| Stock status | How many products are running low or out of stock |

## Add your staff

Go to **Settings > Manage staff > Invite user**. Enter their details and select their role.

### Staff roles

| Role | Who it's for | What they can do |
|---|---|---|
| Admin (owner) | You — the business owner | Everything: all features, reports, users, and settings |
| Manager | A trusted supervisor | Record sales, manage stock, run stock counts, view reports. Can't manage users. |
| Attendant | Counter staff | Record sales, view their own sales, and enter counts during a stock count. Can't see financial reports. |

# Part 2: Feature walkthroughs

---

## 1. Manage your products

### 1.1 Add a new product

1. From the bottom menu, tap **Inventory**.
2. Tap the green **+** button.
3. Fill in the product details:
   - **Product name** (required) — for example, Indomie Chicken 70g
   - **Category** — choose from the list or create your own
   - **Cost price** — what you paid the supplier
   - **Selling price** — what you charge the customer
   - **Opening stock** — how many you have now
   - **Reorder level** — the quantity at which you want an alert
4. Tap **Save product**.

> **Tip:** Tap **More options** to add an expiry date (required for pharmacies).

> **Caution:** If you set the reorder level to 0, you won't receive low-stock alerts for that product.

### 1.2 Record stock arrivals (stock in)

1. Go to **Inventory** and find the product.
2. Tap the product, then tap **Add stock**.
3. Enter the quantity received.
4. Optionally add a note (for example, the supplier invoice number).
5. Tap **Confirm**.

### 1.3 Remove stock manually (stock out)

For stock removed for reasons other than a sale (damage, expiry, personal use):

1. Go to **Inventory** and find the product.
2. Tap the product, then tap **Adjust stock**.
3. Select **Decrease** and enter the quantity removed.
4. Select a reason.
5. Tap **Confirm**.

> **Caution:** Stock-out records are permanent and logged with your name and the time.

---

## 2. Record sales

### 2.1 Record a single-item sale

1. From the dashboard, tap **Record sale**.
2. Type the product name in the search bar and tap the product.
3. Check the quantity (defaults to 1).
4. Tap **Confirm sale**.

> **Tip:** A green tick confirms the sale and the screen clears for the next one.

### 2.2 Record multiple items in one sale

1. Add the first item (steps 1–3 above).
2. Tap **Add another item**.
3. Search and add more products.
4. Tap **Confirm sale**.

### 2.3 Void an incorrect sale

*(Manager or Admin only)*

1. Go to **Sales** in the bottom menu.
2. Find the sale and tap it.
3. Tap **Void sale**.
4. Enter a reason (required).
5. Tap **Confirm void**.

> **Caution:** Void isn't delete. The original sale stays in the record marked as voided, and the stock is returned.

---

## 3. Alerts

### 3.1 Types of alerts

- **Low-stock alert:** fires when stock reaches or falls below your reorder level
- **Expiry alert:** fires before a product's expiry date

### 3.2 Act on a low-stock alert

1. Tap the **Alerts** icon.
2. Find the alert and tap it.
3. Review the current quantity versus your reorder level, then arrange a restock with your supplier.

> **Tip:** Tap **Dismiss** if you've already reordered. The alert won't reappear until stock changes again.

> **Coming soon:** Creating a purchase order in one tap from an alert is on the way, together with supplier management. For now, place your order with your supplier directly.

---

## 4. Run a stock count (reconciliation)

*(Manager or Admin)*

A stock count checks whether what Inventra says you have matches what's actually on your shelf, and shows you what any difference is worth.

### 4.1 Start a count

1. Tap **Reconciliation** (or **More > Reconciliation**).
2. Tap **New Count** to start a session. It's timestamped with your name, and the screen tracks your progress (for example, *50 of 120 counted*).

### 4.2 Enter your physical counts

1. For each product, the screen shows the **system count** (what Inventra expects).
2. Count the item on your shelf and enter the **physical count**.
3. Inventra shows the **difference** and labels each item:
   - **Match** — the counts agree
   - **Loss** — you have fewer than expected
   - **Surplus** — you have more than expected
4. The **value impact** shows what the difference is worth in Naira, and the session shows a total **variance value**.

### 4.3 Resolve a discrepancy

1. Tap an item to open **Product Discrepancy Details**.
2. Check the counts, the value impact, who counted, and when.
3. Type a **reason** (for example, "Broken bottles found on the shelf").
4. Choose one:
   - **Approve Adjustment** — updates your stock to match the physical count.
   - **Request Recount** — sends the item back to be counted again.

> **Caution:** Approving an adjustment updates your stock records and is logged with your name. A confirmation prompt appears first. To correct a mistake later, run a new count or a stock adjustment.

> **Tip:** Use the filter to view items by date range or status — **All**, **Approved**, **Pending**, or **Recount**.

---

## 5. Reports

### View and export a report

1. Tap **Reports** in the bottom menu.
2. Select the report type.
3. Choose a date range.
4. Tap **Preview**.
5. Tap **Export** and choose **PDF** or **CSV**.
6. The file appears in your phone's share menu — save it, or send it by WhatsApp or email.

> **Tip:** PDF is great for accountants. CSV is useful for Excel.

# Part 3: Frequently asked questions

---

## Getting started

**How long does onboarding take?**
From download to recording your first product takes under 10 minutes. Recording your first sale after that takes under 30 seconds.

**Can I use Inventra on a tablet or computer?**
Yes. Open the Inventra web address in Chrome on any device. On Android phones, you can also install it as an app.

---

## Products and inventory

**I have hundreds of products. Do I add them one by one?**
In the current version, yes. Bulk import via Excel/CSV is planned for a later release. Start with your highest-turnover products.

**What is the reorder level?**
The quantity at which Inventra alerts you to reorder. Set it based on your supplier's delivery lead time.

**Can I delete a product?**
You can archive it. All sales and stock history are preserved.

**My stock count went to zero but I can see goods on the shelf. What happened?**
A sale or adjustment was likely recorded incorrectly. Go to **Inventory > [Product] > Stock history** to review all movements, then run a stock count to correct it.

**Can I check my actual stock against what the app says?**
Yes. Use **Reconciliation** to run a stock count. Enter your physical counts, and Inventra shows every difference and what it's worth, so you can approve the correction or ask for a recount.

---

## Sales

**My attendant recorded the wrong quantity. What do I do?**
A Manager or Admin can void the sale and re-record it. Go to **Sales**, find the sale, tap it, and select **Void sale**.

**Can my attendant see financial reports?**
No. Attendants see only their own sales. Reports and revenue summaries are visible to Admin and Manager roles only.

---

## Staff and permissions

**I want staff to record sales but not see my profits. Is that possible?**
Yes. Assign them the Attendant role.

**A staff member left. How do I remove their access?**
Go to **Settings > Manage staff**, find the staff member, and tap **Deactivate**. Access is revoked immediately.

---

## Suppliers and purchase orders

**Can I add suppliers or create purchase orders?**
Not yet. Supplier management and purchase orders are **coming soon** in a later release. For now, manage suppliers and orders outside the app and use stock-in to record what arrives.

---

## Reports

**Can I share a report with my accountant?**
Yes. Generate the report, tap **Export > PDF**, then share it by WhatsApp or email.

---

## Technical and app questions

**I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
On the login screen, tap **Forgot password** and follow the steps to set a new one.
