# Inventra — Demo video script

**Target runtime:** about 3 minutes 50 seconds
**Format:** Screen recording plus voiceover
**Voiceover language:** English (Nigerian accent preferred)
**Background music:** Upbeat and professional, faded under the voiceover, no lyrics
**Audience:** Prospective SME users, investors, pilot participants
**Captions:** English closed captions on all voiceover lines
**Branding:** Inventra logo in the top-right corner throughout; outro card with the website and download link

> **Production note.** Inventory reconciliation, suppliers and purchase orders, phone one-time password (OTP) sign-up, and six-digit personal identification number (PIN) login are all built and can be recorded. The offline background sync engine is not yet built; if an offline scene is included, treat it as "coming soon" rather than a live demonstration.

## Column guide

| Column | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Visual | What appears on screen |
| Voiceover | The exact words the narrator speaks |
| Production action | Instructions for the editor or on-screen graphics |

## Full script

### Scene 01 — 0:00–0:15 | The problem

**Visual:** A busy Nigerian retail shop. A woman at the counter looks stressed, surrounded by paper notebooks and a phone full of messages.

**Voiceover:** Every day, millions of Nigerian shop owners run their businesses from memory, notebooks, and WhatsApp. They lose stock without knowing it. They run out of products while customers are standing right in front of them.

**Production action:** Cinematic shots of a busy market or shop. Warm tones. Fade in the Inventra logo, bottom-right, at 0:10.

### Scene 02 — 0:15–0:25 | Brand reveal

**Visual:** The Inventra logo animates in. Tagline below: Know your stock. Run your business.

**Voiceover:** Introducing Inventra. The smart, simple inventory app built for Nigerian businesses.

**Production action:** Logo animation, then the tagline. Music rises slightly. Hold for two seconds.

### Scene 03 — 0:25–0:45 | Onboarding

**Visual:** The sign-up screen. A hand enters a phone number, receives a one-time password, enters it, and adds a business name. The dashboard loads.

**Voiceover:** Getting started takes minutes. Sign up with your phone number, confirm the code we send you, add your business name, and you are in.

**Production action:** Screen recording, close-up. Highlight each tap.

### Scene 04 — 0:45–1:05 | Dashboard

**Visual:** Dashboard with summary cards: total stock value, low-stock items, pending purchase orders, and monthly sales revenue, plus a priority-alerts panel.

**Voiceover:** Your dashboard tells you exactly what is happening in your business, at a glance. Stock value, low-stock warnings, pending orders, and the items that need your attention, all in one screen.

**Production action:** Highlight each card as the voiceover mentions it.

### Scene 05 — 1:05–1:25 | Inventory

**Visual:** Inventory screen with colour-coded stock badges. The narrator taps a product to show current stock, reorder level, and a low-stock badge.

**Voiceover:** Inventra tracks every product in real time. Green means you have enough. Amber means reorder soon. Red means act now.

**Production action:** Animate the badges. Zoom into the detail screen.

### Scene 06 — 1:25–1:45 | Record a sale

**Visual:** New sale screen. The attendant searches for a product, sets the quantity, and confirms. A green tick appears and the screen clears.

**Voiceover:** Recording a sale takes a few taps. Search for the product, confirm the quantity, and tap confirm. Stock updates automatically.

**Production action:** Highlight each tap. Keep it under 20 seconds.

### Scene 07 — 1:45–2:05 | Staff accountability

**Visual:** Sales list filtered by attendant. Sales appear with product, quantity, time, and amount.

**Voiceover:** As the owner, you can see every sale, by every staff member, for any time period.

**Production action:** Slow pan. Highlight the attendant column.

### Scene 08 — 2:05–2:20 | Suppliers and purchase orders

**Visual:** Supplier list, then a purchase order being created and its status updated.

**Voiceover:** Keep your suppliers in one place, raise a purchase order in a few taps, and track it through to delivery.

**Production action:** Show the supplier list, then the purchase-order flow.

### Scene 09 — 2:20–2:45 | Inventory reconciliation

**Visual:** Stock-count screen. Summary cards show products counted, pending approval, discrepancies, and variance value in Naira. The list shows system count, physical count, difference, and a result of Match, Loss, or Surplus. The narrator opens a discrepancy, reads the reason, and taps Approve Adjustment; a confirmation prompt appears.

**Voiceover:** From time to time, check that your records match your shelves. Inventra puts the system count beside your physical count, flags every difference, and tells you what it is worth in Naira. Approve the adjustment to update your stock, or send it back for a recount.

**Production action:** Highlight the variance card. Colour-code Loss in red and Surplus in green. Show the confirmation prompt.

### Scene 10 — 2:45–3:05 | Alerts

**Visual:** Alerts screen with a low-stock alert and an expiry alert.

**Voiceover:** Inventra warns you before problems happen. Low stock, or a product expiring soon, you get an alert in good time.

**Production action:** Show each alert clearly.

### Scene 11 — 3:05–3:25 | Reports

**Visual:** Reports screen. The owner selects a sales report and a date range; summary cards appear. The owner exports the report.

**Voiceover:** At the end of the week, generate your sales report in seconds. See your revenue, your best-selling products, and your staff performance, then export and share it.

**Production action:** Show the report cards. Highlight the export button.

### Scene 12 — 3:25–3:40 | All personas

**Visual:** Transitions between four shots: owner checking the dashboard, attendant recording a sale, manager reviewing stock, pharmacy owner seeing an expiry alert.

**Voiceover:** Inventra is for every Nigerian business. Whether you are the owner, the manager, or the attendant on the counter, Inventra helps you do your job better.

**Production action:** Each persona gets three to four seconds. Music swells.

### Scene 13 — 3:40–3:50 | Outro

**Visual:** Inventra logo centred. Tagline and download link below.

**Voiceover:** Inventra. Know your stock. Run your business.

**Production action:** Hold the logo. Show the download link or a QR code. Music fades out.

## Pre-recording checklist

- Demo account prepared with at least 20 products, two below reorder level and one expiring soon
- An attendant account with several recorded sales
- At least one supplier and one purchase order
- A reconciliation session with at least one Loss and one Surplus, each with a reason entered
- Phone on Do Not Disturb, notifications hidden except Inventra
- Screen recording software ready (1080p minimum)
