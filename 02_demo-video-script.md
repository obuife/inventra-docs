# Inventra — Demo video script

**Target runtime:** Approximately 3 minutes 50 seconds (3:50). If the offline scene (Scene 10) is cut until the feature ships, runtime returns to roughly 3:30.
**Format:** Screen recording of the Android app plus voiceover
**Voiceover language:** English (Nigerian accent preferred)
**Background music:** Upbeat, professional — fade under VO. No lyrics.
**Audience:** Prospective SME users, investors, pilot participants
**Screen resolution:** 1080p minimum
**Captions:** English closed captions on all VO lines
**Branding:** Inventra logo in the top-right corner throughout. Outro card with website and download link.

> **Production status note**
> Scenes marked **[In development]** show capabilities that aren't in the current build (offline sync). Record those scenes only after the feature ships. Until then, you can cut the launch video without them or use an on-screen "coming soon" treatment.
>
> **Inventory reconciliation (Scene 09) is built and can be recorded now.** Purchase-order creation is **not** in the current build, so it has been removed from the alerts scene — do not demo it until supplier management ships.

## Full script

### Scene 01 — 0:00–0:15 | The problem

**Visual:** Busy Nigerian retail shop. A woman at the counter looks stressed, surrounded by notebook ledgers and WhatsApp messages.

**Voiceover:**
> Every day, millions of Nigerian shop owners run their businesses from memory, notebooks, and WhatsApp. They lose stock without knowing it. They run out of products while customers are standing right in front of them.

**Production action:** Cinematic B-roll of a busy Lagos market or shop. Warm tones. VO enters at 0:02. Fade in the Inventra logo bottom-right at 0:10.

---

### Scene 02 — 0:15–0:25 | Brand reveal

**Visual:** Screen fades to black. The Inventra logo animates in from centre. Tagline appears below: *Know your stock. Run your business.*

**Voiceover:**
> Introducing Inventra. The smart, simple inventory app built for Nigerian businesses.

**Production action:** Logo animation — 1-second fade-in. Tagline types on after the logo lands. Music rises slightly. Hold for 2 seconds.

---

### Scene 03 — 0:25–0:45 | Onboarding

**Visual:** Android phone showing the Inventra sign-up screen. A hand enters an email address and a password. The business-name screen appears — the user types "Mama Ngozi Stores." The dashboard loads.

**Voiceover:**
> Getting started takes less than 3 minutes. Enter your email, set a password, add your business name, and you're in. No complicated forms. Just a few taps.

**Production action:** Screen recording, close-up of the phone. Highlight each tap with a subtle circle. Speed up slightly if needed to fit 20 seconds.

---

### Scene 04 — 0:45–1:05 | Dashboard

**Visual:** Dashboard screen. The greeting reads: *Good morning, Ngozi. You have 2 items running low.* Summary cards show today's sales, stock status (amber), and active alerts.

**Voiceover:**
> Your dashboard tells you exactly what's happening in your business, every morning, at a glance. Today's sales. Low-stock warnings. The items that need your attention — all in one screen.

**Production action:** Highlight the greeting. Highlight each card as the VO mentions it. Slow, deliberate pacing.

---

### Scene 05 — 1:05–1:25 | Inventory

**Visual:** Inventory screen with colour-coded stock badges. The narrator taps *Dettol Soap 135g*. Detail shows current stock 4, reorder level 10, red low-stock badge.

**Voiceover:**
> Inventra tracks every product in real time. Green means you have enough. Amber means reorder soon. Red means act now. Every product shows exactly how many units are left and your reorder level.

**Production action:** Animate the badges as mentioned. Zoom into the detail screen for the last 5 seconds.

---

### Scene 06 — 1:25–1:45 | Record a sale

**Visual:** New Sale screen. A hand types "Indomie." The product appears instantly. The attendant taps it (quantity 1), then taps **Confirm sale**. A green tick: *Sale recorded.* The screen clears.

**Voiceover:**
> Recording a sale takes 3 taps. Search for the product, confirm the quantity, and tap confirm. Stock updates automatically. Your attendant is ready for the next customer in seconds.

**Production action:** Highlight each tap. Make the tick satisfying and confident. Keep the sequence under 20 seconds.

---

### Scene 07 — 1:45–2:05 | Attendant accountability

**Visual:** Sales list. The owner filters by attendant "Amaka." Sales appear with product names, quantities, times, and amounts. The owner taps one for detail.

**Voiceover:**
> As the owner, you can see every sale, by every staff member, for any time period. You see exactly who sold what, when, and for how much.

**Production action:** Slow pan through the list. Highlight the attendant column. End on a transaction detail view.

---

### Scene 08 — 2:05–2:20 | Alerts

**Visual:** Alerts screen with two alerts: *Dettol Soap — low stock* and *Paracetamol Syrup — expiring soon.* The narrator taps the first to view its detail (current quantity versus reorder level).

**Voiceover:**
> Inventra warns you before problems happen. Low stock? You get an alert. A product expiring soon? You get an alert in good time — so you can act before you run out or lose stock to expiry.

**Production action:** Show each alert clearly. Highlight the alert-type icons. (Purchase-order creation is not in the current build — do not show it.)

---

### Scene 09 — 2:20–2:45 | Inventory reconciliation

**Visual:** Reconciliation **Stock count** screen. Summary cards show *Products counted 50/120*, *Pending Approval*, *Discrepancies*, and *Variance value ₦25,000 (potential loss)*. The list shows products with system count, physical count, difference, and a Result of **Match**, **Loss**, or **Surplus**. The narrator taps *Coca-Cola 50cl* (system 7, physical 5, −2, Loss). The **Product Discrepancy Details** sheet opens, showing the value impact (−₦600) and the reason *"Broken bottles found on the shelf."* The narrator taps **Approve Adjustment**, and a confirmation prompt appears warning that stock records will update.

**Voiceover:**
> From time to time, check that your records match your shelves. Inventra's stock count puts the system count right beside your physical count, flags every difference, and tells you exactly what it's worth in Naira. Approve the adjustment to update your stock, or send it back for a recount.

**Production action:** Highlight the variance value card. Colour-code Loss in red and Surplus in green. Pause on the reason field. Show the confirmation prompt before the approval lands. This is a built feature — record it for the launch video.

---

### Scene 10 — 2:45–3:05 | Offline mode **[In development]**

**Visual:** The network bar shows no signal. An **Offline mode** ribbon appears. The attendant records a sale. A sync badge appears: *1 sale waiting to sync.* Signal returns and the badge clears with a tick.

**Voiceover:**
> Even without internet, Inventra keeps working. Sales are saved on the phone and sync automatically when your connection returns. No data is ever lost.

**Production action:** **Record this scene only once offline sync ships.** Until then, omit it or replace it with a "coming soon" card. When ready, give it full screen time — it's a key differentiator.

---

### Scene 11 — 3:05–3:25 | Reports

**Visual:** Reports screen. The owner selects *Sales report, this week.* Summary cards appear: total revenue, top product, transaction count. The owner taps **Export > PDF**, then **WhatsApp**.

**Voiceover:**
> At the end of the week, generate your sales report in seconds. See your total revenue, your best-selling products, and your staff performance. Export to PDF and share it with your accountant on WhatsApp.

**Production action:** Show report cards populating quickly. Highlight the Export button. End on the WhatsApp share action.

---

### Scene 12 — 3:25–3:40 | All personas

**Visual:** Smooth transitions between four shots — owner checking the dashboard, attendant recording a sale, manager reviewing stock, pharmacy owner seeing an expiry alert.

**Voiceover:**
> Inventra is for every Nigerian business — retail shops, pharmacies, wholesalers, mini-marts. Whether you're the owner, the manager, or the attendant on the counter, Inventra helps you do your job better.

**Production action:** Each persona gets 3–4 seconds. Music swells approaching the outro.

---

### Scene 13 — 3:40–3:50 | Outro

**Visual:** Inventra logo centred. Tagline: *Know your stock. Run your business.* Download button / web URL below.

**Voiceover:**
> Inventra. Know your stock. Run your business. Download now.

**Production action:** Hold the logo for 10 seconds. Animate the tagline in. Show the download URL or QR code. Music fades out.

## Narrator notes

| Guidance | Detail |
|---|---|
| Tone | Warm, confident, relatable. Talking to a Nigerian shop owner, not a tech conference. |
| Pace | Steady and clear. Slightly slower than natural speech. No rushing. |
| Accent | Nigerian English preferred. |
| Key words to emphasise | "exactly", "automatically", "instantly", "3 taps", "what it's worth in Naira", "even without internet", "never lost" |
| Pauses | Pause 0.5 seconds after each key benefit. |
| Energy arc | Build from S01 (empathy) → S06–S10 (excitement) → S12–S13 (inspiring close). |
